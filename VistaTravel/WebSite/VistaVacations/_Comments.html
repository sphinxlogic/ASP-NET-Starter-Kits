Comment from Lake Tahoe
Rating: Great

June 15th, 2000 by Ryan Marples

The view was amazaing, I wanted to get up early every day just to see the sunrise. The beaches were great, sandy and clean! There was a great night life. I really enjoyed going to the clubs. Lake Tahoe is a great place for tropical hiking. My family and I spent days in the forest. I enjoyed trying the neat restaurants there. Very nice! I wish I had spent more time, there are so many attractions to see!
